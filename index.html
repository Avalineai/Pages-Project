All About Wolves

The wolf, is also know as the grey/gray wolf or timber wolf, and is a canine native to Eurasia and North America. It is distinguished from its larg size and less pointed features such as ears and muzzle.

Wolves hunt in packs but typically single wolves or mated pairs have higher success rates.

Wolves howl to assemble the pack, to pass an alarm, or to locate each other during a storm or in unfamiliar territory. This is their way of communicating across great distances.
